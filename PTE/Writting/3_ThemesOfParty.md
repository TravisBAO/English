

### 给你的提分小练习
在这个话题中，有一个非常万能的句式，你可以背下来直接用在任何“建议信”里：

 - "It would be highly beneficial to [做某事], as this would [带来的好处]." (做某事将会非常有益，因为这会……)

 - It would be highly beneficial to curate a playlist, as this would create a relaxing environment.

### 修改建议

 - 词汇的精确度： "prepare the music" 比较普通，用 "curate a playlist" 更地道；"special foods" 可以用 "diverse catering options"。

 - 语法的复杂性： 避免使用过多的 "could", "I suggest"，尝试使用虚拟语气或更高级的动词结构。

 - 表达的地道性： "There will be a lot of fun to play..." 语法上略显生硬，建议改为 "This would significantly enhance the social atmosphere..."。
  
 - 句式： 增加“建议类”高级句型，例如 **It would be highly beneficial to...** 或 **I would like to propose that...**。

 - 高级动词 (Action Verbs):

    - curating (策划/精选) 替代 prepare。

    - **cater to** (迎合/满足) 替代 like。

    - **encourage social engagement** (鼓励社交参与) 替代 play with friends。

 - 逻辑连贯词 (Transition Words):

    - 使用 First and foremost (首先且最重要)。

    - 使用 Specifically (具体来说)。

    - 使用 **In addition** (此外)。

 - 语气掌控 (Register):

    - 使用了 It would be ideal to... (这种委婉的建议语气是高级英语的体现)。


**我的答案**

Dear Mrs Singh,

I'm delighted to know the club is going to hold a party. I could provide some practical suggestions for that.

The most important is to prepare the popular music, which could contribute a relaxing environment. I recommend the super star Michael Jackson. Besides that I suggest preparing some special foods, such as various fruits and snacks full of protein, which all the club members like. Lastly we could arrange some small games that everyone could join in. There will be a lot of fun to play with friends and other members.

Please let me know if you need any help. I am more than happy to help prepare the party.

Best regards,

Travis

**Gemini修改的**

Dear Mrs. Singh,

I was delighted to **learn about the upcoming club party** and would like to **offer** some practical suggestions.

First and foremost, I recommend curating a playlist featuring popular tracks from artists like Michael Jackson, which would contribute to a **vibrant atmosphere**. Regarding the catering, **it would be ideal to** provide diverse refreshments, such as **high-protein** snacks and **seasonal fruits**, to cater to our members' health-conscious preferences. Lastly, incorporating **interactive games** would be an excellent way to **encourage social engagement and ensure everyone feels included.**

I am more than happy to **assist with** the preparations. Please let me know if you **require further help**.

Best regards,

Travis
